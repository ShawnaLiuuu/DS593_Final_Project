- 13 - THE CHARLES SCHWAB CORPORATION
MR. SEIP was named Senior Executive Vice President - Retail Brokerage of the Company and Schwab in March 1994. He has been President of Charles Schwab Investment Management, Inc. (CSIM) since July 1992 and Chief Operating Officer of CSIM since June 1991. From July 1992 until March 1994, he was Executive Vice President - Mutual Funds and Fixed Income Products. Mr. Seip has been with the Company since January 1983. Prior to becoming Senior Vice President of the Company and assuming his mutual fund responsibilities in June 1991, Mr. Seip was the divisional executive in charge of Schwab's retail branches east of the Mississippi. From 1985 to 1988, Mr. Seip founded and was responsible for Schwab's Financial Advisors Service. Before joining Schwab, he was a Vice President and Partner at Korn Ferry International.
