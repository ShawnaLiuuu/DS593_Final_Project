Results of Operations 1993 Compared to 1992 - -------------------------------------------
Earnings from continuing operations in 1993 increased $57 million to $127 million. The increase is attributable to the acquisition of Bredero Price, improved earnings in Oilfield Services and Engineering Services operations and changes implemented to reduce costs associated with retiree medical benefit plans.
Revenues increased from $3.8 billion to $4.2 billion. The consolidation of Dresser-Rand's financial statements in 1993 was the primary reason for the increase. In 1992, Dresser-Rand was accounted for using the equity method.
