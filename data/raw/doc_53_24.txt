NOTE 6 - SHORT-TERM BORROWINGS
Total average short-term borrowings during 1993, 1992, and 1991 were $1,038.3 million, $776.9 million, and $662.8 million, respectively. Commercial paper and bank borrowings outstanding at December 31, 1993, generally had maturities not exceeding 90 days with average discount rates of 3.6% and 7.0%, respectively. The approximate weighted average interest rate on short-term borrowings was 4.8%, 5.2%, and 7.2% for 1993, 1992, and 1991, respectively. Interest paid on short-term borrowings was $58.3 million in 1993, $60.3 million in 1992, and $62.3 million in 1991.
