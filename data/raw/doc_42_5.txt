Insurance Underwriting
First Capital is engaged in the property and casualty and accidental death and dismemberment insurance business through Associates Insurance Company ("AIC") and in the credit life, credit accident and health insurance business through Associates Financial Life Insurance Company ("AFLIC"), principally for customers of the finance operations of First Capital. At December 31, 1993, AIC was licensed to do business in 50 states, the District of Columbia and Canada, and AFLIC was licensed to do business in 49 states and the District of Columbia. In addition, First Capital receives compensation for certain insurance programs underwritten by other companies through marketing arrangements in a number of states.
The operating income produced by the finance operations' sale of insurance products is included in the respective finance operations' operating income.
