Under the asset and liability method of FAS 109, deferred income taxes are recognized for the future tax consequences attributable to differences between bases. Deferred tax assets and liabilities are measured using enacted tax rates expected to apply to taxable income in the years in which those temporary differences are expected to be recovered or settled.
