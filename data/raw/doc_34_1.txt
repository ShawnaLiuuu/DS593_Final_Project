Commercial Aircraft Financing Segment
The Company's commercial aircraft financing group, located in Long Beach, California, provides customer financing services to Douglas Aircraft Company, a division of MDC, and finances the acquisition of MDC aircraft by purchasing such aircraft subject to lease to airlines and by providing secured and unsecured notes receivable financing in connection with the acquisition of such aircraft. Beginning in 1986, the Company began providing financing to airlines for aircraft manufactured by manufacturers other than MDC, but a substantial majority of the commercial aircraft portfolio is comprised of aircraft manufactured by MDC.
