COMPENSATION COMMITTEE INTERLOCKS AND INSIDER PARTICIPATION
The Executive Compensation Committee (the "Committee") is comprised of Mr. Bahr, Chairman, and three of the Selex directors, namely, Messrs. Gram, Muyres and Zwaans.
Mr. Bahr, Chairman of the Committee and Vice Chairman of the Board, retired from the Company in December, 1985. From the Company's incorporation in September, 1962 until his retirement, Mr. Bahr served as an officer of the Company and/or its subsidiaries. He presently does not serve as a director or as a member of the compensation committee of any other company.
