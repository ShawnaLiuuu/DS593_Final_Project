Section 4.5. Filings. All actions by or in respect of, and all filings with, any governmental body, agency or official required in connection with the execution, delivery and performance of this Agreement and the Note, or necessary for the validity or enforceability hereof and thereof or for the protection or perfection of the rights and interests of the Bank hereunder and thereunder, will, prior to the date of delivery hereof or thereof, have been duly taken or made, as the case may be, and will at all times thereafter remain in full force and effect.
