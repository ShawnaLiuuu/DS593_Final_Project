During the course of 1992 and 1991 development drilling focused on natural gas wells which qualified under a federal tax incentive program providing for future tax credits on wells producing from tight sands gas formations. To qualify, wells must have been spudded before January 1, 1993. During 1993, development drilling focused on replacing and increasing production capacity as natural gas prices stabilized at acceptable levels.
