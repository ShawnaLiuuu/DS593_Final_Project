(5) Income Taxes
United, its subsidiaries and other affiliated companies file a consolidated federal income tax return with UAL. Under an intercompany tax allocation policy, United and its subsidiaries compute, record, and pay UAL for their own tax liability as if they were separate companies filing separate tax returns. In determining their own tax liabilities, United and each of its subsidiaries take into account all tax credits or benefits generated and utilized as separate companies, and they are compensated for the aforementioned tax benefits only if they would be able to use those benefits on separate company bases.
