The loss during December 31, 1991 included a $660,000 net extraordinary gain from BankAtlantic's early retirement of $10.2 million of Capital Notes by exchanging cash payments, cash bonuses, and Non-Cumulative Preferred Stock ("Preferred Stock") to Capital Noteholders.
