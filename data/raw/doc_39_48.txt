5. STOCK OPTION AND PURCHASE PLANS: - -------------------------------------
Northwest Natural's 1985 Stock Option Plan (Plan) authorizes an aggregate of 300,000 shares of common stock for issuance as incentive or non-statutory stock options. These options may be granted only to officers and key employees of the Company designated by its Board of Directors.

All options granted are at an option price not less than market value at the date of grant and may be exercised for a period not exceeding 10 years from the date of grant. Option holders may exchange shares owned by them for at least one year, at the current market price, to purchase shares at the option price.
During 1985 and 1990, 150,000 and 86,500 options were granted under the Plan at option prices of $17.625 and $24.875, respectively.
