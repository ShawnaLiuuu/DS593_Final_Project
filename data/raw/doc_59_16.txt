On June 19, 1992, the Company completed a transaction with Selex whereby, among other things, Selex loaned the Company $3,000,000 (the First Selex Loan). The First Selex Loan is collateralized by a first mortgage on certain of the Company's property in its St. Augustine Shores, Florida community. The Loan matures on June 15, 1996 and provides for principal to be repaid at 50% of the net proceeds per lot for lots requiring release from the mortgage, with the entire unpaid balance becoming due and payable at the end of the four year term. It initially bears interest at the rate of 10% per annum, with payment of interest deferred for the initial eighteen months of the Loan and interest payments due quarterly thereafter.
