Due to monetary policy changes which resulted in additional interest rate cuts and the continued outflow of time deposits, management decided in September 1992 to dispose of the fixed rate mortgage-backed securities in order to, among other things, fund substantially all of the purchases of these replacement securities. BankAtlantic has the ability and intent to hold its remaining mortgage- backed securities held for investment until their scheduled maturities. Market values of the securities available for sale at December 31, 1993 were greater than BankAtlantic's cost of such securities. All fixed rate mortgage- backed securities having original maturities of 15 to 30 years are classified as available for sale.
