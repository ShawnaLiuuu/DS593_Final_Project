On April 30, 1993 Selex loaned the Company an additional $1,000,000 collateralized by a first mortgage on certain of the Company's property in its Marion Oaks, Florida community (the "Second Selex Loan"). The Second Selex Loan bears interest at 11% per annum, with interest deferred until December 31, 1993. The Second Selex Loan provides for principal to be repaid at $3,000 per lot for lots requiring release from the mortgage, with the entire unpaid principal balance and interest accruing from January 1, 1994 to April 30, 1994 to be due and payable on April 30, 1994.
