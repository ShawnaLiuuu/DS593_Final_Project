Employees - ---------
As of December 3, 1993, the Company employed approximately 3,300 full-time and part-time employees, approximately 1,000 of whom were represented by collective bargaining units. Management believes that its relations with its employees are good. The Company entered into an amendment to a collective bargaining agreement and a new collective bargaining agreement in 1993, in connection with the closing of its distribution center in New Bedford, Massachusetts and the proposed closing of its distribution center in Boston, Massachusetts, respectively.
Environmental Matters - ---------------------
Compliance with federal, state and local regulations with respect to the environment have had, and are expected to have, no material effect on the capital expenditures, earnings or competitive position of the Company.
