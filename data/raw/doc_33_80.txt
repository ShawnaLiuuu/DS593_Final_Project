Contingencies - As discussed in Item 3 and in Note 14 of Notes to the Consolidated Financial Statements, the Company has several contingencies that could impact its liquidity in the event that the Company is unsuccessful in defending against the claimants. Financial Services Business --------------------------
Termination of Assistance Agreement - During the first quarter of 1993, Equity Bank finalized an agreement with the RTC terminating the Assistance Agreement entered into between Equity Bank and the FSLIC in 1988 (the "Assistance Agreement"), in connection with Equity Bank's acquisition of Arrowhead. In connection with such termination, the RTC paid Equity Bank approximately $14.2 million and all of the obligations of both parties under the Assistance Agreement were terminated. As a result of the termination of the Assistance Agreement, Equity Bank assumed the credit risk with respect to approximately $30.8 million of assets (as of the date the Assistance Agreement was terminated ) that had been acquired from Arrowhead in 1988, with respect to which the FSLIC had previously borne the credit risk.
