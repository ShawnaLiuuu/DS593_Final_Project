7. INCOME TAXES
Effective January 1, 1993, the Company adopted Statement of Financial Accounting Standards No. 109, "Accounting for Income Taxes" ("FAS No. 109"). As a result of implementing FAS No. 109, retained earnings for all periods between 1986 and 1992 have been reduced by approximately $63 million from amounts previously reported. The statements of income for those periods subsequent to December 31, 1986 have not been restated as the impact of FAS No. 109 on net income is immaterial to any such year and in total.
