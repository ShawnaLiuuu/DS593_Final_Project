Competition: -----------
The Financial Services Business experiences substantial competition in attracting and retaining deposits and in making loans. The primary factors in competing for deposits consist of the ability to offer attractive rates and the availability of convenient office locations. Competition for financial services historically comes from other savings institutions, commercial banks and credit unions. However, securities firms and mortgage companies are also competitors.
