From August 2, 1993 through September 30, 1993, the Trust operated as debtor-in-possession and incurred net reorganization expenses of $679,000. For the year total revenues (excluding reorganized revenue items, which equaled $27,000 or 0.1% of prior year's revenue) decreased by 14.9%. Rental revenues declined by $2,862,000 (10.4%), and interest income declined by $1,809,000 (46.7%).
Total expenses (excluding reorganization expense items, which equaled $706,000 or 1.6% of prior year's expenses) decreased by $3,772,000 (8.7%) from 1992 to 1993.
