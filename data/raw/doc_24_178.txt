BankAtlantic adopted FAS 109 as of January 1, 1993. The cumulative effect of this change in accounting for income taxes was immaterial, and thus, there was no cumulative effect adjustment. In accordance with FAS 109 deferred tax liabilities are not recognized on the base year tax bad debt reserve unless it becomes apparent that the reserve will be reduced and result in taxable income in the foreseeable future. At December 31, 1993, BankAtlantic's base year tax bad debt reserve was $6.6 million for which no deferred income taxes have been provided.
