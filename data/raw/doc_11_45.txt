The following supplemental data on the Company's oil and gas activities were prepared in accordance with the Financial Accounting Standards Board's (FASB) Statement of Financial Accounting Standards No. 69 - "Disclosures About Oil and Gas Producing Activities." Estimated future net cash flows are determined by: (1) applying the respective year-end oil and gas prices to the Company's estimates of future production of proved reserves; (2) deducting estimates of the future costs of development and production of proved reserves based on the assumed continuation of the cost levels and economic conditions existing at the respective year-end; and (3) deducting estimates of future income taxes based on the respective year-end and future statutory tax rates. Present value is determined using the FASB-prescribed discount rate of 10% per annum.
