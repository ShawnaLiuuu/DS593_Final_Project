ITEM 10. TRUSTEES, EXECUTIVE OFFICERS AND ADVISOR OF THE REGISTRANT
Trustees
The affairs of National Income Realty Trust (the "Trust" or the "Registrant") are managed by a ten-member Board of Trustees. The Trustees are elected at the annual meeting of shareholders or appointed by the incumbent Board of Trustees and serve until the next annual meeting of shareholders or until a successor has been elected or approved.
The Trustees of the Trust are listed below, together with their ages, terms of service, all positions and offices with the Trust or its advisor, Basic Capital Management, Inc. ("BCM" or the "Advisor"), or Tarragon Realty Advisors, Inc. ("Tarragon"), the Trust's advisor commencing April 1, 1994, their principal occupations, business experience and directorships with other companies during the last five years or more. The designation "Affiliated", when used below with respect to a Trustee, means that the Trustee is an officer, director or employee of BCM or Tarragon or an officer or employee of the Trust.
