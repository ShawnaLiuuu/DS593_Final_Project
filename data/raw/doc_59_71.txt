From July 9, 1993 through December 31, 1993, Selex loaned the Company an additional $4,400,000 collateralized by a second mortgage on certain of the Company's property on which Selex and/or Yasawa hold a first mortgage pursuant to a Loan Agreement dated July 14, 1993 and amendments thereto (the "Third Selex Loan"). The Third Selex Loan bears interest at 11% per annum, with interest deferred until December 31, 1993. Principal is to be repaid at $3,000 per lot for lots requiring release from the mortgage, with the entire unpaid principal balance and interest accruing from January 1, 1994 to April 30, 1994 becoming due and payable on April 30, 1994. As of August 31, 1994, accrued interest of $466,800 due under the Third Selex Loan as well as the principal balance of $4,384,200 remained unpaid and in default.
Interest due to Selex, Yasawa and their affiliates in the aggregate amount of $2,300,000 remained unpaid and in default as of August 31, 1994.
