On a same property basis, rental revenues remained flat during the year, although in the third and fourth quarter, the Trust began to see some strengthening in the leasing markets in most of the areas in which the Trust operates in terms of both traffic and rental rates (other than in Southern California). Same property occupancy improved to 89% at December 31, 1993 from 88% at December 31, 1992.
The Trust terminated its Advisory Agreement with the Advisor effective June 12, 1993. In accordance with the terms of the Advisory Agreement, a one-time termination fee of $435,000 was paid to the Advisor on June 12, 1993. The Trust is now self-administered and employs six full-time employees to conduct and administer the business affairs of the Trust.
