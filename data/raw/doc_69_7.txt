Seasonality
TropWorld experiences seasonal fluctuations in casino play that management believes are typical of casino hotel operations in Atlantic City. Operating results indicate that casino play is seasonally higher during the months of May through October; consequently the Company's revenues during the first and fourth quarters have generally been lower than for the second and third quarters and from time to time the Company has experienced losses in the first and fourth quarters. Because TropWorld's operating results are especially dependent upon operations in the summer months, any event that adversely affects the operating results of TropWorld during such period could have a material adverse effect on the Company's operations and financial condition. Given Atlantic City's location, it is also subject to occasional adverse weather conditions such as storms and hurricanes that would impede access to Atlantic City, thus adversely impacting operations. The gaming markets in Las Vegas and Laughlin experience a slight decrease in gaming activity in the hot summer months and during the holiday period between Thanksgiving and Christmas.
