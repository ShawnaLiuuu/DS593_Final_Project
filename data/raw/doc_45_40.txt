Note 14
DIVIDEND RESTRICTIONS
The approval of the appropriate regulatory authority is required if the total of all dividends declared by a subsidiary bank in any calendar year exceeds the
bank's net profits (as defined) for that year combined with its retained net profits for the preceding two calendar years. In 1994, the subsidiaries may declare dividends without regulatory approval of $7.8 million plus an additional amount equal to the net profits of the Company's subsidiary banks for 1994 up to the date of any such dividend declaration.
