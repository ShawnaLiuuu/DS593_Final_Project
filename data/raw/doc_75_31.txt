Future development and production costs are estimated expenditures to develop and produce the proved reserves based on year-end costs and assuming continuation of existing economic conditions. Future income taxes are calculated by applying appropriate statutory tax rates to future pre-tax net cash flows from proved oil and gas reserves less recovery of the tax basis of proved properties, and adjustments for permanent differences.
