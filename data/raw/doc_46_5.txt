THE NET PROFITS ROYALTIES
The Net Profits Royalties entitle the Trust to receive, on a quarterly basis, 90% of the Net Proceeds (as defined in the Net Profits Conveyances) from the sale of production from the Net Profits Properties. The Net Profits Royalties are not limited in term, although under the Trust Agreement the Trustee is directed to sell the Net Profits Royalties prior to the Liquidation Date.
