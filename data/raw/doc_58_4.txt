To the Shareholders of The Midland Company:
We consent to the incorporation by reference in Registration Statement No. 33-48511 of The Midland Company on Form S-8 of our report dated February 10, 1994, incorporated by reference in this Annual Report on Form 10-K and our report (appearing below) on the financial statement schedules of The Midland Company for the year ended December 31, 1993.
Our audits of the consolidated financial statements referred to in our aforementioned report also included the financial statement schedules of The Midland Company and its subsidiaries, listed in Item 14. These financial statement schedules are the responsibility of the Company's management. Our responsibility is to express an opinion based on our audits. In our opinion, such financial statement schedules, when considered in relation to the basic consolidated financial statements taken as a whole, present fairly in all material respects the information set forth therein.
