Covered Assets
Prior to the Termination Agreement, FSLIC had guaranteed the December 30, 1988 book value of covered assets, as defined, of Arrowhead, under the provisions of the Assistance Agreement. Covered assets were defined to include all Arrowhead assets acquired except cash and marketable securities, performing one to four family residential first mortgage loans, certain fixed assets and assets owned by subsidiaries.
