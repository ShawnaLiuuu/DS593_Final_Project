Note 8. Accounts Payable
Accounts payable at December 31, 1993 and 1992, included liabilities in the amount of $304 million and $342 million, respectively, for checks issued in excess of related bank balances but not yet presented for collection.
Note 9. Long-Term Debt
Amoco's long-term debt resides principally with two Amoco subsidiaries--Amoco Company and Amoco Canada. Amoco Company functions as the principal holding company for all of Amoco's petroleum and chemical operations, except Canadian petroleum operations and selected other activities.
