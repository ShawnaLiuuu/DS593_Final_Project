The terms of the debt restructuring prohibit the Trust from making distributions to shareholders until all principal and capitalized interest scheduled for payment through December 31, 1993, have been paid. No distributions were made by the Trust for the fiscal years ended September 30, 1993 and 1992.
The Trust is in default under the debt restructuring agreement due to, among other factors, its filing a petition under Chapter 11 (see Note 1).
