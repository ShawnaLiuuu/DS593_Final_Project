5. COMMON STOCK
On September 14, 1993 the Board of Directors of the Company declared a two-for-one stock split in the form of a 100 percent stock dividend effective October 15, 1993. The stock split resulted in an increase in common stock and a reduction in additional paid-in capital of $56.6 million. All share and per share data, except as otherwise indicated, have been restated to give retroactive effect to the stock split.
On March 8, 1993 the Company sold 4,025,000 shares of common stock at $68.88 per share, on a pre-split basis.
