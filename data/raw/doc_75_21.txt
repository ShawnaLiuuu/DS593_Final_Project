Note 15. Taxes
Effective January 1, 1992, the Corporation adopted Statement of Financial Accounting Standards ("SFAS") No. 109. The cumulative effect of the accounting change, relating to years prior to 1992, was to increase deferred income tax liabilities as of January 1, 1992, and reduce net income by $68 million ($.14 per share). In addition, 1992 net income before the cumulative effect was $215 million ($.43 per share) greater than it would have been under SFAS No. 96, the previous method.
