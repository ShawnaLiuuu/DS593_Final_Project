8. Affiliates' Participation in Joint Ventures:
(a) The Brookhollow/Stemmons Center Office Complex is owned by a joint venture between the Partnership and an affiliated partnership. Profits and losses are allocated 72.5% to the Partnership and 27.5% to the affiliate.
(b) The Perimeter 400 Center Office Building is owned by the Partnership and three affiliated partnerships. Profits and losses are allocated 50% to the Partnership and 50% to the three affiliates.
(c) The Sun Lake Apartment Complex is owned by the Partnership and an affiliated partnership. Profits and losses are allocated 61.95% to the Partnership and 38.05% to the affiliate.
All assets, liabilities, income and expenses of the joint ventures are included in the financial statements of the Partnership with the appropriate adjustment of profit or loss for each affiliate's participation.
