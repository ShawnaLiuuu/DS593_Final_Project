Natural Gas
The System operating companies have various long-term gas contracts that will satisfy a significant percentage of each operating company's needs; however, such contracts typically require the operating companies to purchase less than half of their annual gas requirements under such contracts. Additional gas requirements are satisfied under less expensive short-term contracts and spot-market purchases.
