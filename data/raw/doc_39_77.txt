The system includes written policies, procedures and guidelines, an organization structure that segregates duties and an established program for monitoring the system by internal auditors. In addition, Northwest Natural Gas Company has prepared and annually distributes to its management employees a Code of Ethics covering its policies for conducting business affairs in a lawful and ethical manner. Ongoing review programs are carried out to ensure compliance with these policies.
The Board of Directors, through its Audit Committee, oversees management's financial reporting responsibilities. The committee meets regularly with management, the internal auditors, and representatives of Deloitte & Touche, the Company's independent auditors. Both internal and external auditors have free and independent access to the committee and the Board of Directors. No member of the committee is an employee of the Company. The committee reports the results of its activities to the full Board of Directors. Annually, the Audit Committee recommends the nomination of independent auditors to the Board of Directors for shareholder approval.
