PART II - Assignment of Life Insurance Policy as Collateral
A. For value received and in specific consideration of the premium payments made by the Employer as set forth in Section B of Part I hereof, the Employee hereby assigns, transfers and sets over to the Employer (herein in this Part II called the "Assignee"), its successors and assigns, the Policy issued by the Insurer upon the life of Employee and all claims, options, privileges, rights, titles and interest therein and thereunder (except as provided in Paragraph C hereof), subject to all terms and conditions of the Policy and to all superior liens, if any, which the Insurer may have against the Policy.
