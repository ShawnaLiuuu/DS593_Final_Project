Capital Investments. The Company is implementing a hotel development and acquisition program, which focuses on its proprietary limited-service brands, Wellesley Inns and AmeriSuites, and on strategically positioned full-service hotels. In November 1993, the Company opened its newly constructed Wellesley Inn in Orlando, Florida. The Company is constructing a new Wellesley Inn in the Sawgrass section of Fort Lauderdale, Florida and has begun development of a Wellesley Inn site in Lakeland, Florida. The Company plans to acquire and convert two additional Wellesley Inns in 1994. The Company has also purchased a site in Tampa, Florida for planned construction of an AmeriSuites hotel. The Company plans to develop two additional AmeriSuites in 1994. The Company is also evaluating opportunities to acquire and rehabilitate existing full-service hotels either for its own portfolio or with investors.
