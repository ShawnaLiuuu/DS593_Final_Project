State. The State of Illinois imposes a corporate income tax of 4.8% and a replacement tax of 2.5% on the Illinois net taxable income of savings banks. For tax years beginning after June 30, 1993, the corporate income tax rate will be 4.4%. Illinois taxable income is substantially similar to federal taxable income with certain adjustments (including the addition of interest income on state and municipal obligations and the exclusion of interest on United States Treasury obligations). In 1993, 1992, and 1991, the exclusion of income on United States Treasury obligations had the effect of reducing significantly the Illinois taxable income of St. Paul Federal.
