NOTE 7. INTEREST RATE SWAP AGREEMENT
The Company had outstanding an interest rate swap agreement with a commercial bank. This agreement had a notional principal amount of $50,000,000 and matured on December 31, 1991. Under the terms of the agreement, the Company made annual interest payments to the bank based on a fixed rate of 12.41%, and the bank made quarterly interest payments to the Company based on the LIBOR rate.
