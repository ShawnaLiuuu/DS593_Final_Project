NOTE 9: SUBSEQUENT EVENTS
On January 25, 1994, the Board of Trustees adopted an Incentive Share Plan (the 'Plan'), which provides for the issuance of up to 1,500,000 Common Shares to outside Trustees, officers and key executives of the Company through options to purchase Common Shares, share appreciation rights, and restricted share grants. Common Share options may be options that are intended to qualify as incentive options under the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended, or options which are not intended to so qualify. Options will be granted with an exercise price that approximates the fair value of the Common Shares on the grant date.

Certain officers also received in aggregate 5,950 restricted Common Shares.
