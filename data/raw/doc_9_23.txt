In the consolidated statement of cash flows for 1993, other changes related to operations principally included $106 million of payments for Alaska tax and geothermal energy sales contract settlements. Also included was a cash flow reduction of $125 million relating to the settlement of crude oil forward sales contracts, for which revenue was recognized in 1993, but cash was received in 1992.
The consolidated statement of cash flows for 1992 excluded the effect of noncash activities related to the merger of Unocal Exploration Corporation into Union Oil (see Note 17). The effect on the balance sheet was to increase properties, deferred income taxes and stockholders' equity by $173 million, $64 million and $142 million, respectively, and to decrease minority interest liability by $33 million.
