In March 1994, an agreement was entered into to settle the litigation pertaining to the 1991 Exchange. Pursuant to the agreement, the company will pay approximately eighty-one percent (81%) of the face amount of the outstanding debentures held by plaintiffs and the debentures will be canceled pursuant to the procedures outlined in the agreement. The settlement is subject to, among other things, court approval. Upon effectiveness, the settlement of this action will be dismissed with prejudice and the parties will exchange releases.
Other lawsuits have been filed against the Company in connection with the Exchange offers. The Company is pursuing discussions with the remaining plaintiffs in litigation relating to the Exchange offers with a view to settling the ongoing litigation but there is no assurance that a settlement will be reached.
