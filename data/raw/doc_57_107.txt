The ability of the Company to make capital distributions is restricted by the limited cash resources of the Company and the ability of the Company to receive dividends from the Association. The Association's payment of dividends is subject to regulatory limitations, particularly the prompt corrective action regulation, which prohibits the payment of a dividend if such payment would cause the Association to become undercapitalized. Also, the Company and the OTS entered into a Dividend Limitation Agreement as a part of the holding company approval process which prohibited the payment of dividends to the holding company without prior written OTS approval if the Association's capital is below its fully phased-in capital requirement or if the payment of such dividends would cause its capital to fall below its fully phased-in capital requirement.
