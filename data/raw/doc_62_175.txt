Except as provided in or as otherwise consistent with the provisions of this Agreement, the Employer covenants that it will not exercise its rights under the Collateral Assignment provisions of this Agreement in such a manner as to defeat the rights of the Employee or the policy beneficiary under this Agreement. Specifically, the Employer covenants that it will not surrender the Policy unless Part I of the Agreement has terminated as provided in Section G and there has been a default in Employee's obligation under Section H of this Part I. The Employer shall have possession of the Policy during the period that the Employer makes premium payments and until all amounts due the Employer are repaid. The Employer shall make the Policy available to the Insurer in order to make any change desired by the Employee as to the designation of beneficiary or the selection of a settlement option, subject, however, to the provisions of this Agreement and the Collateral Assignment.
