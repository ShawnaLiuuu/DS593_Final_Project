The Bank's Credit Review Department evaluates the risk ratings of all multi-family, commercial and 1-4 family mortgage assets on a 9-class scale and, in 1994, will begin to risk rate home equity and other consumer loans. The thirty-two factors in the multi-family and commercial rating system are evaluated under one of the five categories following: property management, financial risks, operating risks, the competitiveness of the marketplace and the condition of the collateral. The 1-4 family rating system uses a matrix with 9 risk factors, heavily weighted toward borrower creditworthiness.
