NOTE 6. COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH RESTRUCTURING OPERATIONS
In the second quarter of 1993, the Company recorded a restructuring charge of $451,000 for consolidation of CooperSurgical facilities and related reorganization and relocation costs.
NOTE 7. SETTLEMENT OF DISPUTES
The Company and CLS entered into a settlement agreement, dated July 14, 1993, pursuant to which CLS delivered a general release of claims against the Company, subject to exceptions for specified on-going contractual obligations, and agreed to certain restrictions on its acquisitions, voting and transfer of securities of the Company, in exchange for the Company's payment of $4,000,000 in cash and delivery of 200,000 shares of common stock of CLS owned by the Company and a general release of claims against CLS, subject to similar exceptions. See Note 15 for a discussion of the settlement terms. The cash paid and fair value of CLS shares returned to CLS were charged to the Company's statement of operations for 1993 as settlement of disputes. In addition, the Company charged $1,500,000 to the statement of operations for certain other disputes.
