On December 2, 1992 the Company entered into various agreements relating to certain of its assets and the restructuring of its debt with Yasawa. The consummation of these agreements was conditioned upon the acquisition by Mr. Gram of the bank debt under the Sixth Restatement as described above. On December 4, 1992, Gram acquired the Bank Loan of approximately $25,150,000 (including interest and fees) for a price of $10,750,000, as well as the warrants which the lenders held. Immediately thereafter, Gram transferred all of his interest in the Bank Loan, including the warrants, to Yasawa.
