The following methods and assumptions were used by the Company in estimating its fair value disclosures for financial instruments:
Cash and certificates of deposit: The carrying amount reported in the balance sheet for cash and certificates of deposit approximates its fair value.
Long- and short-term debt: The fair values of the Company's Credit Agreement loans are estimated using indicative market quotes obtained from a major bank. The fair values of senior notes, senior debentures, senior subordinated notes, senior subordinated discount debentures, subordinated discount debentures, the sinking fund debentures, and the junior subordinated debentures are based on indicative market quotes obtained from a major securities dealer. The fair values of other loans approximate their carrying value.
