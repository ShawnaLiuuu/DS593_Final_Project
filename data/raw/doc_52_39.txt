NOTE 9 -- RETIREMENT AND PROFIT SHARING PLAN:
During 1993, the Trust adopted a retirement and profit sharing plan which qualifies under section 401(k) of the Internal Revenue Code. All existing Trust employees at adoption and subsequent employees who have completed one year of service are eligible to participate in the plan. The Trust may make annual discretionary contributions to the plan. Plan contributions by the Trust in 1993 were $12,000.
