THE COMPANY AS LESSEE
In 1990, the Company entered into a 30-year lease, with three, six-year renewal options, on the Plaza, an adjacent 9-acre site on which there is situated an industrial building that has been renovated and converted into retail shopping space. The Plaza opened for business in September 1991. In addition to specified rents, the Plaza lease requires the Company to pay property taxes, insurance, operating expenses and additional rentals based on a percentage of revenues generated by the operations of the Plaza. No such additional rentals were paid in 1993, 1992 or 1991. In accordance with applicable accounting standards, the portion of the lease related to the building is accounted for as a capital lease while the portion related to the land is accounted for as an operating lease.
