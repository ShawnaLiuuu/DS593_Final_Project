The Partnership owned an 80% general partnership interest in The Oaks Community located near Sarasota, Florida. During the fourth quarter of 1991, the Partnership's joint venture partner failed to make capital contributions required to fund ongoing operations and pursuant to the joint venture agreement, was in default of the agreement. The Partnership executed an agreement in August 1993 with its joint venture partner, CIS Oaks, Ltd., ("CIS"), whereby CIS assigned its 20% interest in The Oaks to the Partnership, thereby vesting 100% control of the assets of the joint venture in the Partnership.
Certain of the assets of The Oaks joint venture were encumbered by two mortgage loans. A $12,492,200 loan was scheduled to mature in January 1997 and a $3,260,000 loan was scheduled to mature in December 1993. The joint venture had guaranteed $2.7 million of the loans, and the guaranteed amount was with recourse to the Partnership.
