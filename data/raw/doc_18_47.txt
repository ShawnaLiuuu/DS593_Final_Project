Imo Industries Inc. and Subsidiaries Notes to Consolidated Financial Statements
Note 1 Significant Accounting Policies
Basis of Presentation: The accompanying financial statements have been prepared assuming the Company will continue as a going concern. The Company currently anticipates that cash flow from operations together with cash generated by asset sales will be sufficient to meet the required repayment of its senior debt by September 30, 1994 (see Note 8). Without the sale of a sufficient portion of its assets currently held for sale or the refinancing of its senior obligations, the Company would not have sufficient cash flow from operations to meet this repayment requirement. The process of disposing of assets is continuing and in addition, management is presently seeking replacement financing. Although significant progress has been made to date, and management believes its plans are achievable, there can be no guarantee as to the Company's ability to sell sufficient assets or obtain refinancing within the necessary time frame.
