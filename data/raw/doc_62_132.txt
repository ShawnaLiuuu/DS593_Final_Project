Note F - Postretirement Benefits Other Than Pensions:
The Company adopted SFAS No. 106 as of January 1, 1993, which requires accrual of postretirement benefits other than pensions (principally health care and life insurance) for the employee and covered dependents during the years the employee renders the necessary service to receive such benefits. Prior to 1993, medical expenses and life insurance premiums paid by the Company for retired employees and their dependents were recorded in expense in the period in which they were paid and were $1,907,000 and $1,721,000 in 1992 and 1991, respectively.
