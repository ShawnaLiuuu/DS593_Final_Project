On January 1, 1994, the Company adopted SFAS No. 115, "Accounting for Certain Investments in Debt and Equity Securities." It classified its marketable securities as available-for-sale and recorded an unrealized post- tax holding gain of $34.2 million, net of a tax effect of $23.0 million, in a separate component of shareholders' equity. There was no effect on net income as a result of this adoption.
