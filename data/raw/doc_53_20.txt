NOTE 1 - SUMMARY OF SIGNIFICANT ACCOUNTING POLICIES
A. Operations and basis of consolidation
Caterpillar Financial Services Corporation (the "Company") is a wholly owned finance subsidiary of Caterpillar Inc. ("Caterpillar"). The Company provides financing of earthmoving, construction, and materials handling machinery and engines sold by Caterpillar dealers, and turbine engines sold by Solar Turbines Incorporated through offices located in North America, Australia, and Europe. The Company also provides customer and dealer loans for various business purposes.
The accompanying financial statements include the accounts of Caterpillar Financial Services Corporation and its foreign subsidiaries.
